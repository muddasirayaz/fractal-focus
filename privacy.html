Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2, 2026 — Last Updated: April 2, 2026

Fractal Focus by Amanah Digital Health

1. Overview

Fractal Focus ("the App") is a Pomodoro productivity timer published by Amanah Digital Health. We built this app with a privacy-first philosophy: your data stays on your device. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

The App stores the following data locally on your device only, using your browser's localStorage or the device's equivalent sandboxed storage:

• Timer settings (focus duration, break duration, number of rounds, sound/vibration preferences)
• Session history (session type, duration in minutes, timestamp of completion)
• Aggregate statistics (total sessions completed, total minutes focused)

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to us, to any server, or to any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporters, or tracking SDKs. The App does not use the Apple IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers). The App does not use any fingerprinting or device-identification techniques.

The App loads Google Fonts (DM Sans, JetBrains Mono) from Google's CDN. Google may collect standard web request logs (IP address, browser type) as part of serving these fonts. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. No other external network requests are made.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with any third party. There is no data to share because all data remains on your device.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device for as long as the App is installed. You can delete all App data at any time by:

• Using the "Reset All Data" button in the App's Settings screen, which immediately and permanently erases all session history and statistics
• Uninstalling the App, which removes all locally stored data
• Clearing your browser's storage/cache (for the web version)

Once deleted, data cannot be recovered because it was never stored externally.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Because no personal data is collected from any user, no special provisions for children's data are required. The App does not include third-party advertising or third-party analytics.

7. User Consent and Permissions

The App does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, health data, photos, or any other device sensor or personal data store. The only device API used is the Vibration API (for timer completion haptic feedback), which does not collect or transmit any data. Vibration can be disabled in the App's Settings screen.

8. Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device using the platform's sandboxed storage, it is protected by your device's own security measures (device passcode, biometric lock, encryption at rest). We do not have access to this data.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top and, for material changes, notify users through the App's update notes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights under laws such as the GDPR, CCPA, or equivalent regulations. Because we do not collect or process personal data on our servers, most data-subject requests (access, portability, erasure) are satisfied by the fact that your data is entirely under your control on your device. If you have questions or wish to exercise any rights, contact us at the address below.
