Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2, 2026

Fractal Focus is a privacy-first application built by ContentFlip. We believe your focus data is yours alone.

1. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not require an account, email address, or any other identifying information to use the application.

2. No Cookies or Tracking

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party analytics services. No behavioral data is sent to external servers.

3. Local Data Storage

All focus sessions, statistics, and user settings are stored locally on your device using localStorage. This data remains on your browser and is never uploaded to a cloud or shared with third parties.

4. Data Control

Since we do not collect any data on our servers, we have no data to delete or provide. You can wipe all local application data at any time using the "Reset All Data" button within the app's Settings screen.
